Bird
1. Foraging and Hunting: Birds have evolved unique foraging strategies, from seed-eating finches to predatory hawks and eagles. 2. Nesting and Breeding: Birds build intricate nests, often using plant material, mud, and other natural materials. 3. Roosting and Sleeping: Many birds roost in large groups, often in trees or on cliffs, and have unique sleeping patterns, such as sleeping with only half their brain at a time.
